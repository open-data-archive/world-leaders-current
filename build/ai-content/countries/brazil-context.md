## Brazil — Government System

Brazil is a presidential federal republic. The President serves as both the head of state and head of government, elected by direct popular vote for a four-year term with the possibility of one consecutive re-election. The Vice President is elected on the same ticket.

The National Congress is bicameral, consisting of the Federal Senate and the Chamber of Deputies. Brazil is a federation of 26 states and one Federal District (Brasilia), each with its own elected governor and state legislature.
