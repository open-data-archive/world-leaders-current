## Laos — Government System

The Lao People's Democratic Republic is a single-party socialist republic governed by the Lao People's Revolutionary Party (LPRP). The President serves as the head of state and the Prime Minister serves as the head of government. Both are elected by the National Assembly.

The National Assembly is the country's unicameral legislature. Candidates for the National Assembly are vetted by the LPRP. The party's Central Committee and Politburo play central roles in policy direction and governance of the country.
