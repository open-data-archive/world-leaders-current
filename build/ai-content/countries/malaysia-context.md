## Malaysia — Government System

Malaysia is a federal parliamentary constitutional monarchy. The Yang di-Pertuan Agong (King) serves as the head of state, selected on a rotational basis from among the nine hereditary state rulers for a five-year term. The Prime Minister is the head of government and is appointed by the King from the majority coalition in Parliament.

Parliament is bicameral, comprising the Dewan Rakyat (House of Representatives) and the Dewan Negara (Senate). Malaysia is a federation of 13 states and 3 federal territories, with each state having its own constitution and legislature.
