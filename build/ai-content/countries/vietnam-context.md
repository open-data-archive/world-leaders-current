## Vietnam — Government System

Vietnam is a single-party socialist republic governed by the Communist Party of Vietnam (CPV). The President serves as the head of state, while the Prime Minister serves as the head of government. Both are elected by the National Assembly, the country's unicameral legislature.

The Communist Party of Vietnam holds the leading role in the state and society as defined by the constitution. The General Secretary of the CPV is a key leadership position. The National Assembly elects or approves senior government officials and passes legislation.
