## Government System

## Australia — Government System

Australia is a federal parliamentary constitutional monarchy and a Commonwealth realm. The monarch of Australia (shared with the United Kingdom and other Commonwealth realms) is the head of state, represented domestically by the Governor-General. The Prime Minister is the head of government, appointed by the Governor-General as the leader of the party or coalition holding a majority in the House of Representatives.

The Federal Parliament is bicameral, comprising the Senate and the House of Representatives. Australia is a federation of six states and two mainland territories, each with its own parliament and government.
