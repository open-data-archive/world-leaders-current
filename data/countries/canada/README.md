## Canada — Government System

Canada is a federal parliamentary constitutional monarchy and a Commonwealth realm. The monarch of Canada (shared with the United Kingdom and other Commonwealth realms) is the head of state, represented in Canada by the Governor General. The Prime Minister is the head of government, serving as the leader of the party or coalition holding a majority in the House of Commons.

The Parliament of Canada is bicameral, consisting of the appointed Senate and the elected House of Commons. Canada is a federation of ten provinces and three territories, each with its own legislature and premier or commissioner.
