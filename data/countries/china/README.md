## China — Government System

The People&#39;s Republic of China is a single-party socialist republic governed by the Chinese Communist Party (CPC). The President serves as the head of state, while the Premier of the State Council serves as the head of government. The General Secretary of the CPC holds the most senior leadership position within the party.

The National People&#39;s Congress (NPC) is the unicameral national legislature and is constitutionally the highest organ of state power. The State Council, led by the Premier, functions as the chief administrative authority. China is divided into provinces, autonomous regions, municipalities, and special administrative regions.
