## Government System

## Indonesia — Government System

Indonesia is a presidential republic. The President serves as both the head of state and head of government, elected directly by the people for a maximum of two five-year terms. The Vice President is elected on the same ticket as the President.

The legislature, the People&#39;s Consultative Assembly (MPR), consists of two chambers: the People&#39;s Representative Council (DPR) and the Regional Representative Council (DPD). Indonesia is a unitary state divided into provinces, each with its own elected governor and legislature.
