## Government System

## Singapore — Government System

Singapore is a parliamentary republic. The Prime Minister is the head of government and holds executive power, while the President serves as the head of state in a largely ceremonial role with some custodial powers over national reserves and key appointments. The President is elected by popular vote for a six-year term.

The unicameral Parliament is the legislative body, with members elected through a mix of single-member and group representation constituencies. The Prime Minister is the leader of the party or coalition that commands a majority in Parliament.
