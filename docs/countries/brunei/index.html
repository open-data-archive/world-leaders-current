## Brunei Darussalam — Government System Brunei Darussalam is an absolute monarchy in Southeast Asia. The Sultan serves as both the head of state and head of government, holding executive authority. The country does not hold general elections, and political parties are banned. The Sultan governs with the assistance of appointed councils, including the Privy Council, the Council of Ministers, and the Legislative Council. The legal system incorporates both common law and Islamic (Sharia) law. Brunei has been ruled by the same royal dynasty, the House of Bolkiah, since the 14th century.
