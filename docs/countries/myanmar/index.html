Government System

## Myanmar — Government System Myanmar's governance structure has been in a state of dispute since the military seized power in February 2021. The State Administration Council (SAC), led by the military, has exercised governmental authority since that time. Prior to 2021, Myanmar operated as a parliamentary republic with a President as head of state and a bicameral legislature. The 2008 constitution established a parliamentary system with reserved military representation in the legislature. Leadership of the country remains contested, with both military authorities and opposition groups claiming governmental legitimacy. The situation continues to evolve.
