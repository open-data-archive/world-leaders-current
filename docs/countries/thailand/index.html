Government System

## Thailand — Government System Thailand is a parliamentary constitutional monarchy. The King serves as the head of state and holds a revered position in Thai society, while the Prime Minister serves as the head of government. The Prime Minister is selected by the House of Representatives and formally appointed by the King. The National Assembly is bicameral, consisting of the House of Representatives (lower house) and the Senate (upper house). Members of the House of Representatives are elected by the public. Thailand's current governmental framework is based on the 2017 constitution.
